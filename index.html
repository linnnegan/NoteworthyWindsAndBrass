WHAT IS NOTEWORTHY WINDS AND BRASS?

Noteworthy Winds and Brass is a small family owned shop specializing in the restoration of woodwind and brass instruments. While piccolos, flutes and clarinets are our specialty, our technician has spent over 30 years working on all instruments including saxophones and all brass (french horn specialist) instruments. Ultrasonic tank on the premises, we can do both rotor and piston valve and trombone slide alignments, no job is too big or too small.
