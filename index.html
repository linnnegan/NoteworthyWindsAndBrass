NOTEWORTHY WINDS AND BRASS

Noteworthy winds and brass is an instrument repair and restoration buisnesses. expert woodwind work, with a specialty in flute, piccolo, and clarinet restoration. noteworthy winds and brass also does brass repairs; including rotor and piston valve, as well as trombone slide alignments. with an ultrasonic tank on the premises, no job is too big or too small.
